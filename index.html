About US

Reviving kerala is an initiative to bring transparency, traceability and efficiency to disaster relief and rehabilitation programs using latest technologies in order to prevent : 

    Mismanagement and theft of relief supplies

    Improper implementation of long term rehabilitation programs

Why Reviving Kerala

Contributions coming from different parts of world are not reaching out to deserving people at remote corners of flood and landslide affected areas of Kerala due to the improper distribution, theft and mismanagement of responsible government officials, biased political, religious and community workers.
Thousands of tonnes of relief materials are piled up at different relief camps and collection centers at different parts of kerala due to the lack of coordination with victims and relief volunteers.
Rehabilitation process including rebuilding shelter, livelihood and community tends to follow the same pattern of variance as we have no medium to verify transactions reaching out to deserving people

How it works

There is no technology solution to encompass assessment of damages  and remote  validation
of relief and rehabilitation programs with end to end traceability and transparency.
Existing legacy systems are not capable of providing authenticity for contributions and donations reaching out deserving and needy.

Reviving kerala provides a mobile and web based platform for contributors, relief workers, NGO organizations and governments to trace out contributions and victims who are receiving it

Intelligent Damage Assessment

Physical assesment using AR and GPS, Victims data will be collected from relief centers and via govt officials and validated via Aadhar 

Traceble Supplies 

The contributions send to the respective places can be tracked via GPS which ensures the supplies are getting to the right place

Transparent Donations

All contributions made in terms of physical goods, Cash , Digital currencies and all assets are transparent for public and can even trace the destiation of assets. 

Unmuttable Ledger

All contributions made are resistant to all kind of attacks means the contribution history will remain safe over there for lifelong.
